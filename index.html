Get started.

Call 979-701-8830 to schedule an appointment.

We are open Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 1:00 PM to 5:00 PM.
Closed Saturday and Sunday.

When you come in for your appointment, your Case Manager will complete an assessment with you. During this process, we will determine the areas where we can assist you and then create a Resource Access Plan to address them.

We require that you bring the following documents to your appointment:

  • ID for anyone in the household that is 18 years or older.
  • Social security cards for all household members.
  • Birth certificates for all household members (if born in the U.S.).
  • Proof of residency for all household members (if born outside of the U.S.).
  • Proof of income (such as a current year award letter or pay stubs for the past 30 days, unemployment, child support, etc.).
  • Your most recent electric bill.
  • Your most recent natural gas or propane bill (if applicable).
  • Your most recent water bill (if applicable).
